Doom Metal

Doom Metal is characterized by its slow tempos, heavy riffs, and melancholic atmosphere. It often features themes of despair and existential dread. Bands like Candlemass and Sleep are examples of bands from this subgenre.

Sludge Metal

Sludge Metal combines elements of Doom Metal and Hardcore Punk, characterized by its heavy, distorted sound and aggressive vocals. Bands like Eyehategod and Crowbar are key figures in this subgenre.
