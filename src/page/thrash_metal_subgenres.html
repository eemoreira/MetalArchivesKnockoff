Subgenres

Crossover Thrash

Crossover Thrash blends elements of Thrash Metal and Hardcore Punk, characterized by its fast tempos and aggressive sound. Bands like D.R.I. and Suicidal Tendencies are key figures in this subgenre.

Blackened Thrash

Blackened Thrash combines elements of Black Metal and Thrash Metal, known for its raw sound and aggressive riffs. Bands like Sodom and Aura Noir are prominent in this subgenre.

Groove Thrash

Groove Thrash incorporates elements of Groove Metal into Thrash Metal, characterized by its heavy, rhythmic riffs and slower tempos. Bands like Pantera and Lamb of God are influential in this subgenre.
