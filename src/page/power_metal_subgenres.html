Symphonic Power Metal

Symphonic Power Metal combines traditional power metal with orchestral elements, often featuring choirs and symphonic arrangements. Bands like Nightwish and Epica are notable in this subgenre.

Progressive Power Metal

Progressive Power Metal incorporates complex song structures and progressive rock elements, characterized by intricate guitar work and varied time signatures. Bands like Ancient Bards and Dream Theater are key figures in this subgenre.

Folk Power Metal

Folk Power Metal blends traditional folk music elements with power metal, often featuring folk instruments and themes. Bands like Korpiklaani and Finntroll are prominent in this subgenre.
